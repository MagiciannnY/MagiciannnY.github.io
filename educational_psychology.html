Educational Psychology

Introduction

Educational psychology is the study of how people learn, including topics such as student outcomes, the instructional process, individual differences in learning, gifted learners, and learning disabilities.

Theories and Models

In educational psychology, various theories and models are used to understand the learning process. Some of the most influential theories include behaviorism, cognitivism, and constructivism.
